I believe in a S.M.A.R.T. approach

The idea is to check the project against these criteria to determine if it's appropriate and well developed.
Specific
Specific enough to convey the full scope.
Measurable
A clear indicator of when the project is complete.
Attainable
Is the project possible?
Realistic
Is it possible to complete the project given the timeline and resources?
Timely
Will the project be useful if it is completed at its deadline?
